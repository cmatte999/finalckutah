F.A.Q

How Regularly Should Carpets Be Professionally Cleaned?

It's best to professionally clean your carpets every 6 to 12 months, especially in high-traffic areas, to maintain their look and hygiene.

Is Pre-Cleaning Vacuuming Necessary?

No need to worry about pre-cleaning vacuuming – our team handles it with our high-grade commercial vacuums for optimal results.

What Should I Do to Get Ready for a Carpet Cleaning Session?

Clear the area by removing small items like toys and light furniture off the carpets, and we'll handle the rest.

Are Your Carpet Cleaning Methods Safe for Kids and Pets?

Absolutely! We use a bio-based, non-allergenic HOST cleaning solution, ensuring safety for both pets and children.

How Long Will It Take for My Carpets to Dry Post-Cleaning?

Our efficient cleaning methods mean your carpets will be ready in just a few minutes, unlike conventional methods that take 6 to 12 hours.

Can All Stains Be Removed from Carpets?

While we effectively remove most stains, certain types like those from bleach or dyes might be challenging. Some deep-seated odors may persist due to liquid penetration into the carpet backing.
